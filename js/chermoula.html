Zesty Chermoula Marinade

A vibrant, herbaceous North African sauce and marinade, perfect for elevating fish, grilled meats, or roasted vegetables.

0 of 10 ingredients prepped

Ingredients Breakdown

1. The Fresh Herbs

  • 1 large bunch fresh cilantro, finely chopped
  • 1 large bunch fresh flat-leaf parsley, finely chopped

2. Aromatics & Spices

  • 4 cloves garlic, minced or mashed
  • 1 tbsp ground cumin
  • 1 tbsp sweet paprika
  • 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper (optional)
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

3. Liquids & Acids

  • 1/3 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 3 tbsp fresh lemon juice (about 1 lemon)
  • 1 tbsp preserved lemon rind, finely chopped (optional)

Preparation Instructions

  1. Prep the Herbs: Wash and thoroughly dry the cilantro and parsley. Finely chop the leaves and tender stems, discarding the thick, woody bottom stems.
  2. Mash the Aromatics: In a mortar and pestle (or a sturdy bowl), crush the minced garlic with the kosher salt to create a smooth paste.
  3. Combine the Spices: Add the cumin, paprika, and cayenne pepper to the garlic paste. Mix well to release the essential oils from the spices.
  4. Mix the Base: Transfer the spice and garlic mixture to a medium-sized bowl. Add the chopped herbs and preserved lemon rind, stirring to combine.
  5. Emulsify: Slowly pour in the olive oil and fresh lemon juice while stirring constantly until the sauce comes together into a thick, vibrant green dressing.
  6. Rest & Serve: Let the chermoula rest at room temperature for at least 30 minutes before using to allow the flavors to meld. Use immediately as a marinade for fish, or store in a jar in the fridge for up to a week!
