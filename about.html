About Us

Cooking Tutorial was created to make learning how to cook simple, accessible, and enjoyable for everyone. Whether you're a beginner or exploring new cuisines, our platform helps guide you every step of the way.

Our Project

Welcome to Cuisine Tutorial an interactive cooking website built as our CS430 final group project. Our goal is to help beginners and food enthusiasts explore global cuisines in a simple and engaging way. Each cuisine features three signature dishes with descriptions and step-by-step tutorials designed to build confidence in the kitchen.
